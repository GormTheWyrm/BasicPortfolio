About Me

As you can probably tell, I am a medieval enthusiast who likes to participate in various medieval activities such as the events run by the Society of Creation Anachronism. Unsurprisingly, I am also a huge fan of medieval fantasy and similarly themed filk and folk music. My other hobbies include sailing, rowing, hiking, swordplay, bonfires and carrying loot on long beachside journeys.

At the time of this writing the SCA website is down, but eventually you should be able to see local events at the Atlantian Calandar of Events.

Recently I enrolled at the University of Richmond for a Web Development Bootcamp. As an aspiring digital creator I spend a lot of my free time learning about Game Design, Story Structure, and Medieval Societies. I am also working on an elaborate fantasy setting for a piece of creative fiction. Hopefully that will be established as the setting for a series of games or novels sometime in the future.

To the horror of all, I have also begun to poke around the idea of using blender for 3D modeling. I am nearly skilled enough to create an egg.

If you are reading this because you are looking to hire me, feel free to believe that I merely forgot to change this after posting it for a school assignment. It could be true. When I am not raiding I can be found by hitting the Contact button.
Or you can try smoke signals.

May your stories entertain, your journeys be joyful, and your mead warm.
